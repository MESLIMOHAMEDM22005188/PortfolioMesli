ENTERPRISE NETWORK

Advanced Multi-Site Network Infrastructure

Complete enterprise topology composed of a Datacenter, two remote agencies and a dual-ISP backbone. The project reproduces real-world routing, wireless and security constraints.

  • BGP (multi-AS, redundancy, failover)
  • DHCP centralization & IP helper
  • WPA2 / WPA2-Enterprise with RADIUS
  • QoS (SIP, RDP, traffic classification)
  • Enterprise Wi-Fi (WLC + APs)

Topology: Datacenter + 2 Agencies + 2 ISPs
Hardware: ISR 2911, Catalyst 3650, WLC 2504, AP 3702i
Protocols: BGP, DHCP, RADIUS, QoS
